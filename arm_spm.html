The main functions of the workstation

AWP "Retail" 2.0 (tariff "Supermarket") is software for automating the work of supermarkets or chain stores. Work is provided with branches, the exchange of invoices, a central warehouse, work with several retail outlets from one workplace.

The interface is available in Ukrainian and Russian. Possibility of cloud and local storage of the database.

The program is fully licensed, and does not require the purchase of additional software from other manufacturers (except MS Windows).
We provide a wide range of trading equipment: scanners and barcode printers, receipt printers, fiscal registrars, POS terminals, scales with label printing and much more, which allows the printing of price tags and labels in one click.

An individual selection of equipment and software for a particular store is possible.

Tariff Features:

  • Tariff for large supermarkets (more than 2 cash registers) and chain stores.
  • It is possible to visit a specialist to install software and train staff.
  • It is possible to refine reports and program functionality.
  • Remote access to reports from anywhere in the world, from any device.
  • Support for fiscal technology and the availability of an online store, as well as the function of composite products and the "Restaurant" mode are available.
  • Functions available for chain stores.
  • Technical support by email and phone.

Features of the program:

  • Product information, unlimited number of fields (cost, margin, date of arrival, expiration date, minimum balance, etc.)
  • Work with invoices. Income, expendables, return invoices. Revaluation acts (automatic, manual). Deferred revaluation.
  • Fixing the cost of each product. expiration date, etc.
  • Inventory: with TSD and without, inventory without closing, analysis of totals, local inventory.
  • Cash desk control, offsets with suppliers, other expenses, salary.
  • Calculation of bonuses to employees, profit of sellers.
  • Discount programs. Cards, cumulative discounts, seasonal and temporary discounts on goods and categories. Selling on credit.
  • Access gradation, unlimited number of users.
  • Work with branches, exchange of invoices, central warehouse, work with several retail outlets from one workplace.
  • Allows you to store your data anywhere - on a local computer, on the network, on a remote server in any country in the world. You are protected from unauthorized access to your data, even in case of equipment removal.
  • Support for discount card readers, printing price tags on a regular printer, working with the customer’s display.
  • Work with scales with label printing, as well as connected to a PC. Direct (from the program) loading of scales.
  • Support for POS keyboards, touch displays, etc.
  • Work with data collection terminals.
  • Bank transfer, work with POS terminals.

The program has a unique function - automatic connection to the ean13.info database , containing more than 9,000,000 items with a bar code. Accordingly, when filling the base (during the opening of the store, or when receiving new goods), the need to manually enter the name of the product practically disappears, which significantly reduces the operator’s working time.
