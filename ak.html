About company

Our company sets as its top priority the production of high-quality and guaranteed safe confectionery products by maintaining the necessary conditions for its production and with strict observance of the requirements of legislative acts and internal regulatory documents, as well as in accordance with the requirements of international standards of the quality management system ISO 9000 and ISO 9001, and also food safety management systems ISO 22000. The company has a developed quality and safety control system as part of of raw materials and finished products at all stages of production, as evidenced by the corresponding certificate and markings on the passage of voluntary certification of products in the Standard Donbass system.
