The main functions of the workstation

Equipment

Our software supports a wide range of trading equipment: Scanners and barcode printers, receipt printers, fiscal registrars, POS terminals, label printing scales, and much more.

Residue control

At any time, you know how much and what kind of product is left in your warehouse. Flexible reports on the goods, various types of invoices allow you to quickly and effortlessly carry out any operation with the goods.

Cash control

You see how much your store earns from anywhere in the world. Everything is taken into account - from mutual settlements with suppliers to the current cash desk of a cash desk and cashless payments. Access statistics online.

Price tags and labels

Printing price tags and labels in one click, both directly from the program and in conjunction with third-party software.

Adaptation

Thanks to a wide range of settings, the software easily adapts to the specifics of your business. Products, household chemicals, household goods, clothes - this is far from an incomplete list of stores where our software is used. The program can easily be used to update the assortment of an online store.

Access Control and Security

Allow users to perform only those functions that they need. All user actions are recorded in reports. The possibility of cloud storage of the database will protect your information from unauthorized access, even in the event of equipment removal.
